Our Mission

Here at Hats for Cats, we don't just make your cats look absolutely adorable.
The health and wellness of cats all over the country is something we care very deep about. That is why 50% of ALL our proceeds go to the Humane Society of the United States. The money goes to making sure cats, as well as other animals, are fed, get the medical attention they may need and fight for animal rights.
